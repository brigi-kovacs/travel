Intro

One thing you need to know about me: I love to travel! No phisical posessions are more valuable to me that to visit a new place, no hobby brings me more joy than that. This is why, for all my birthdays since I'd moved to London, instead of a gift , I asked for a trip somewhere. You also need to know, that I am not a wealthy person, so all my trips, so far, have been inside Europe. Unfortunately, I will spend my 30th birthday in lockdown, due to the 2020 Coronavirus pandemic.I won't let this bring me down- I will find a nice park , where we are alowed to go for a walk or exercise, get a cake and have a nice time with my boyfriend. However, as soon as we can , we will make up for it.
